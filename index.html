About The Anime

Love, Chunibyo & Other Delusions mixes romance, comedy, slice-of-life, and fantasy imagination scenes into a unique anime experience. The exaggerated magical battles are visual representations of the characters' fantasies and emotions.

3D Fan Experience

The animated objects in the background are rendered using Three.js and WebGL. Floating stars, glowing dimensional rings, and rotating energy forms create a fantasy atmosphere inspired by the series.
